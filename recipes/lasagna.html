World's Best Lasagna

This World's Best Lasagna recipe is a rich and flavorful dish that combines layers of perfectly seasoned meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese, all enveloped in tender pasta. The secret to its exceptional taste lies in the savory beef mixture that includes a combination of ground beef and Italian sausage, sauteed with onions, garlic, and seasoned with classic Italian herbs like basil and oregano. The sauce is simmered for hours, allowing the flavors to meld together into a hearty, tangy masterpiece. The lasagna also features a luxurious ricotta cheese mixture, enriched with eggs, parsley, and a generous amount of mozzarella for that gooey, cheesy goodness. The layers are carefully constructed, starting with a rich layer of meat sauce, followed by noodles, then the creamy ricotta mixture, and topped with more mozzarella and Parmesan for an irresistible cheesy crust. Baked to perfection, this lasagna achieves a golden, bubbly top with a deliciously tender interior. Ideal for family gatherings or special occasions, this lasagna is sure to become a favorite thanks to its balance of flavors and satisfying texture. It's a timeless comfort food that promises to impress at any dinner table, with each bite offering a perfect harmony of meat, cheese, and pasta.

Ingredients

  • Meat, round. Sweet Italian sausage.
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna according to recipes
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving.
